Our Services

package_2 SwiftSend

SwiftSend is responsible for composing, formatting, and delivering emails efficiently. It supports rich text editing, attachments, email scheduling, and bulk sending. The service integrates with SMTP providers like Gmail, Outlook, and custom mail servers to ensure reliable email delivery while tracking sent status and failures.

inbox InboxFlow

InboxFlow handles incoming emails, categorizing them for better organization and real-time notifications. It integrates with IMAP/POP3 protocols to fetch emails from different providers while supporting threaded conversations, spam detection, and advanced search filters. Attachments preview and AI-based spam filtering enhance user experience.

mop CleanMail

CleanMail manages email deletion by allowing soft deletes (moving emails to Trash) and permanent removal. It supports bulk deletion and retention policies, ensuring that deleted emails can be recovered within a set timeframe before being erased permanently.

restore_from_trash TrashGuard

TrashGuard maintains deleted emails in a secure, temporary storage area before permanent deletion. Users can restore emails within a retention period, and automated cleanup policies help manage storage efficiently. Logging and audit trails ensure transparency in email recovery.

shield_locked ShieldMail

ShieldMail enhances email security with encryption, spam filtering, and phishing protection. It supports end-to-end encryption, two-factor authentication (2FA), and role-based access control to prevent unauthorized access. Advanced threat detection ensures safe email communication.
